Pedri

Pedri, born Pedro González López on November 25, 2002, in Tegueste, Tenerife, Spain, is a talented Spanish footballer who plays as a midfielder for FC Barcelona and the Spanish national team. From an early age, Pedri displayed exceptional talent, joining local clubs and eventually making his

way to the Las Palmas youth academy in 2018. By 2019, he had signed a professional contract with Las Palmas and debuted with the first team, quickly becoming a standout for his vision and skill. In September 2019, Barcelona secured his transfer, and he began to make his mark on the main squad by the 2020-21 season, earning praise for his technical abilities and strategic play.

Pedri's style on the field draws frequent comparisons to FC Barcelona legends Xavi Hernandez and Andrés Iniesta, known for their intelligent playmaking and composure under pressure.
